About MBIRE TV ZIMBABWE

Welcome to MBIRE TV ZIMBABWE, your trusted independent online media platform broadcasting from the heart of Zimbabwe to the world. We are committed to uncovering local and national stories, providing real-time video coverage, and offering a platform for voice and expression across the region.

Our Mission

To deliver unbiased, comprehensive, and up-to-the-minute news coverage. MBIRE TV acts as a transparent mirror to society, ensuring accountability, educating the community, and providing interactive news submission options for citizens to speak on real situations in their neighborhoods.

Independent Coverage

We pride ourselves on our editorial independence. MBIRE TV is not aligned with any political party or state interest. We rely on organic citizen journalism and public broadcasts to bring you the truth directly from the ground.

Connect With Us

For breaking news tips, inquiries, or promotion opportunities, you can chat with our team directly via WhatsApp or join our fast-growing community channel on YouTube.
